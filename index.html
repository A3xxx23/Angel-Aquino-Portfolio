About Me

Hello, I am Angel Emilio Aquino, a Systems Engineering student at the Universidad Pedro Henríquez Ureña (UNPHU) in Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic.

From an early age, technology has been one of my greatest passions, and about three years ago I discovered my particular interest in programming, especially in the area of Frontend development. Although during my university studies I have had the opportunity to explore both the Backend and the Frontend, in the last year I have dedicated myself to deepening my knowledge in software development, focusing mainly on the frontend. My goal is to reach a level of excellence in this field, with the intention of continuing to climb and master every aspect of frontend development.
